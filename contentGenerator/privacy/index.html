Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 04.06.2025

1. What Data Do We Collect?

We collect only necessary information such as user prompts, model responses, and page content. If you register, we also store your login email and hashed password for authentication purposes.

2. Purpose of Data Usage

Collected data is used to generate meaningful content based on your input. If you register, your previous queries and generated responses will be stored for personal access and history tracking. We do not share your data with any third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is transferred securely using HTTPS. For registered users, data is securely stored in a cloud database (Supabase). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

4. User Rights and Choices

You may choose not to register and use the extension without storing any information. Registered users may contact us anytime to delete their data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use GroqCloud and HuggingFace APIs to generate responses. No personal data is shared with these services beyond the required prompts.

6. Changes and Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. All changes will be reflected on this page and optionally shown inside the extension.
